About the Global Systems Programme

Global Systems is a Master of Science in Engineering programme at Chalmers University of Technology focused on understanding and analysing complex interactions between technology, society, and natural systems.

The programme combines mathematics, physics, computer science, data analysis, modelling, and systems thinking to develop the ability to analyse large-scale challenges and create data-driven solutions.

Through project-based courses and interdisciplinary problem solving, I have developed skills in system analysis, mathematical modelling, programming, simulation, and understanding how different components interact within complex systems.

The programme prepares engineers to work with areas such as sustainable development, data science, artificial intelligence, socio-technical systems, energy systems, and other complex engineering challenges.
